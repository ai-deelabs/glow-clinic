Questions

Common Questions

Your initial consultation is a private, one-to-one session with one of our qualified practitioners — typically 45–60 minutes. It includes a thorough skin analysis, a review of your medical history, and a candid conversation about your concerns and goals. There is no obligation to proceed with any treatment.

This varies considerably depending on the treatment and your individual skin. Some results are visible after a single session; others — particularly for skin restoration or deeper resurfacing — require a course of treatments. Your clinician will provide a realistic, honest timeline during your consultation.

Comfort levels vary by procedure. Many clients describe laser and peel treatments as a warm or tingling sensation rather than pain. Where appropriate, we use topical anaesthetics to ensure the experience is as comfortable as possible. Your clinician will always talk you through what to expect beforehand.

Recovery depends on treatment intensity. Light procedures like superficial peels or photon therapy involve no downtime. Deeper laser treatments may involve redness and peeling for 5–10 days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and remain available throughout your recovery period.
